Stable Operations

Assess disconnected legacy systems, map and connect processes and sources of data—for incremental performance improvements.

OUTCOMES: Increased Compliance, Cost Savings

Talent
  • Manual, human-only workforce—performing transactional work.

  • Workforce: specialized technology and process experts.
Tech
  • Automation Identification Diagnostic Tool (AIDT) desktop app analyses tasks, helps identify transactional work, and creates an optimized process blueprint.

  • Siloed or incomplete data sources unable to gain strategic insights or extract meaningful value.
Data
  • Aggregated data at organization level, unable to blend data to generates forward-thinking insights.

Future-ready Operations

Intelligent operations run end-to-end digitized processes; continually mobilize specialized talent, innovative technology, and diverse data on demand, realizing stronger performance gains and overall value.

  • Real-time Agile Workforce On-demand: Collaborative workforce, ability to mobilize specialized talent (tapping ecosystem partners) when needed.

  • Specialized talent: Knowledge workers focused on judgement-based work, data scientists and innovation leads.
Tech
  • AI, cloud and blockchain enabled.

  • Specialized talent: Knowledge workers focused on judgement-based work, data scientists and innovation leads.
Data
  • Harness insights from a diverse portfolio of sources—using advanced analytics and AI capabilities—to power predictive decisions and improve decision making.
